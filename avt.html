Ashta Veerattanar Temples

Lord Shiva did 8 valorous deeds in 8 different places called Ashta- Veera lakshnam or Ashta Veerattanar Sthalam.

 
1. Bramhasirakandeeswarar Temple Thirukandiyur Bramha
2. Amirthakadeeswarar Temple Thirukadaiyur Yaman
3. Veeratteswarar Temple Thirukkurukai Manmadhan
4. Veeratteeswarar Temple Vazhuvur Gajaasuran
5. Veeratteeswarar Temple Thiruvirkudi Jalandarasuran
6. Veeratteeswarar Temple Thiruvatikai Tripuraanthakan
7. Veeratteeswarar Temple Keezha Parasalur Dakshan
8. Veerattaneeswarar Temple Thirukovalur Andhakaasuran

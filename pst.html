Pancha Sabai Temples

Pancha Sabhai Sthalangal refers to the temples of Lord Nataraja, a form of Lord Shiva where he performed the Cosmic Dance. Pancha indicates Five, Sabhai means hall and Stala means place. All these temples are located in Tamil Nadu. The temples are

 
1. Chidambareswarar Temple, Chidambaram Aananda Thandavam Porchabai Gold
2. Sundareswarar Temple, Madurai Sundhara Thandavam Vellisabai Silver
3. Nellaiyappar Temple, Tirunelveli Bramha Thandavam Thaamirasabai Copper
4. Kutralanathar Temple, Kuttralam Asabhaa Thandavam Chitrasabai Picture
5. Vada Aaranyeeswarar Temple, Thiruvalangadu Uruthuva Thandavam Rathinasabai Emerald

Vishnu

Om Vishnave Namaha

 
Color   Crystal While (Sphatika)
Shakthi (Lakshmi Roopa)   Shanthi
Dress   Gold colored bright garments richly adorned with ornaments
Presiding Deity   Savitr
 

 

As Lord is omnipresent having all the 'Gunas' and He is the Supreme Being He is called 'Visnu'. Visnu Murthi is fifth among Vyuhantara and the second to emanate from Sankarshana. He will be holding Lotus and Conch on the upper left and right hands and Club and Disc on the lower left and right hands. He is the Governing power of the  letter 'Va' of Gayathri Mantra, the letter 'Ga' of Mahamantra, 'Karna Tattva'  one of the 24 Tattvas and Urdhvapundra marked on the right side of the abdomen during the light half of the lunar month. He is the custodian of the Milk,'Simharasi' and the lunar month of 'Chaitra'. He dwells in the "Gandharinaadi"in the mind and in the sacred plant of 'Tulasi'. He is to be meditated on at the time of drinking water. He is invoked in the lower abdomen (right) of the devotee. His flag bears the emblem of the lofty palm tree and a plough.

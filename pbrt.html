Pancha Bramha Temples

According to Hinduism, Pancha Bramha Temples refers to 5 temples on the banks of River Aarani representing 5 faces of Lord Shiva The temples are

 
1. Vaaleeswarar Temple, Ramagiri Issanam
2. Pallikondeeswarar Temple, Suruttappalli Thathpurusham
3. Sampangi Pitchaleeswarar Temple, Aarani Vaamadevam
4. Varamoortheeswarar Temple, Ariyathurai Sathyojatham
5. Chinthamaneeswar Temple, Karungaali Aghoram

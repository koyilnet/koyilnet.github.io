Saptha Sthanam Temples

This Saptha Sthanam Temples refers to 7 temples in and around Thiruvaiyaru related with the marriage of Nandhi - Divnine Bull and vahana of Lord Shiva

 
1. Panchanatheeswarar Temple, Thiruvaiyaru Marriage Place
2. Aabhatsahayeeswarar Temple, Thirupazhanam Fruits collected for marriage
3. Neiyadiyappar Temple, Thillaisthanam Ghee collected for marriage
4. Pushpavananathar Temple, Thirupoonthurutti Flowers collected for marriage
5. Veerattanar Temple, Thirukandiyur Jewels collected for marriage
6. Chottruthurainathar Temple, thiruchottruthurrai Food prepared for the marriage
7. Vedhapureeswarar Temple, Thiruvedhikudi Priest came from here for the marriage
